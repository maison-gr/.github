## 🚀 Our Digital Ecosystem

### 1. Resistaa — The ADHD Operating System
**Status:** 🟢 Live in Production | **Explore at:** [www.resistaa.com](https://www.resistaa.com)

Resistaa is a beautifully designed, comprehensive productivity platform built specifically for adults experiencing executive dysfunction and task-initiation challenges. Instead of adding planning overhead, Resistaa centers its experience around the exact moment a user feels stuck.

*   **AI Unblock Engine:** Identifies specific cognitive friction patterns—such as task paralysis, perfectionism, or time blindness—and breaks down overwhelming tasks into 2–3 realistic, gentle micro-steps.
*   **ADHD Workspace:** A calm, unified dashboard featuring fluid mood and energy tracking, custom daily prompts, configurable focus timers, and unstructured mental brain dumps.
*   **Body-Double Feature:** Provides a supportive virtual co-working presence to help users maintain focus and seamlessly bridge the gap into active task engagement.
*   **Global Accessibility:** Fully localized and optimized natively across English, French, and Arabic.

---

### 2. Reps — AI Conversation Training Platform
**Status:** 🟢 Live in Production | **Explore at:** [www.reps.maisongr.com](https://www.reps.maisongr.com)

Reps is an immersive conversation simulator designed to help individuals practice high-stakes real-world communication in a safe, dynamic environment. Practicing with Reps ensures you are ready before stepping into the room.

*   **Dynamic Roleplay Interactive:** Allows users to simulate 18 crucial scenarios across work and life—including salary negotiations, delivering difficult feedback, landlord disputes, and relationship boundaries.
*   **Real-Time Pushback:** AI characters adapt dynamically to your tone, communication style, and empathy level across three difficulty modes (*Supportive*, *Realistic*, or *Combative*).
*   **Coaching Debriefs:** Delivers an instant personalized review after every session, featuring an objective Readiness Score, aggregate benchmarking, an analysis of what worked versus what fell flat, and actionable first-person alternatives.

---

### 3. Vela — AI-Powered Canadian Recruitment SaaS
**Status:** 🟡 Under Active Construction / Available for Acquisition | **Explore at:** [https://velarecruitment.ca](https://velarecruitment.ca)

Vela is a bilingual recruitment platform designed to seamlessly connect Canadian talent pools with scaling businesses through transparent, accountable matching metrics.

*   **Deterministic Match Scoring:** Provides candidates and employers with clear, math-driven alignment scores from 0 to 100 based on core skills, experience, location, and language capabilities.
*   **Ghost Score Transparency:** Tracks employer response frequencies against Canadian benchmarks, giving candidates real insight into company accountability before they apply.
*   **Collaborative Workspaces:** Outfits recruitment teams with interactive visual pipelines, tailored Kanban tracking boards, and Employment Equity Act filtering options.
*   **Progressive Guest Gating:** Features a fluid public job directory mapping locations and filter parameters seamlessly to maximize candidate acquisition and platform growth.
